# EmbodiedNet VB1 Sample (Version v2)

**Real samples from one representative property** — full six-modality stack.

This verification batch lets you explore native 14-bit RAW stills, 4K log-encoded ego video, HDR brackets & merges, exterior RAW, aerial motion, and metric floor plans.

## Property Information
- **Verification Batch ID**: VB1  
- **Version**: v2  
- **Property Reference**: 297  
- **Capture Date**: November 12, 2025  
- **Location**: Colorado, USA  
- **Property Type**: Single-family home (two-story)  
- **Conditions**: Overcast winter day with soft ambient lighting and generic staging

## Capture Equipment
**Interior**  
- Camera: Sony ILCE-7SM3 (A7S III)  
- Lens: Sigma 14-24mm f/2.8 DG DN Art (primarily at 14 mm)  
- Stills: 14-bit RAW (Sony ARW), Neutral profile  
- Video: 4K S-Log3 (Sony S&Q mode), Display P3  

**Exterior / Aerial**  
- Platform: DJI Air 3  
- Stills: RAW DNG  
- Video: 4K HEVC D-Log M  

All files preserve native sensor characteristics and original embedded metadata.
